Smooth Sweet Tea

A Refreshing tea suitable for cold conditions. Perfect in evenings.

The Recipe is very Easy to make! You must definitly try this evening drink for sure.

  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 6 tea bags
  • Â¾ cup white sugar
  • 6 cups cool water

Steps

  1. A pinch of baking soda into a 64-ounce, heat-proof glass pitcher. Pour in boiling water and add tea bags. Cover and allow to steep for 15 minutes.

  2. Remove tea bags and discard; stir in sugar until dissolved. Pour in cool water; refrigerate until cold, about 3 hours.
